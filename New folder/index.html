About Us

Returning Home is a comprehensive guide for Lithuanian parents and children who are returning from living abroad. Our aim is to help you navigate the challenges of returning home and provide practical advice on everything especially on enrolling your children in school.

Resources

The website https://www.mokykla2030.lt/ provides information about primary and secondary schools in Lithuania. It offers a directory of schools, including public and private institutions, with details such as location, contacts, and educational programs offered. The website also provides general information about the education system in Lithuania, including requirements for enrollment and the structure of the curriculum. Additionally, it offers resources for parents and students, such as guidance on selecting a school and information on available scholarships and grants.

The website https://www.renkuosilietuva.lt/en/primary-and-secondary-schools/ is a useful resource for parents of students who have returned to Lithuania after studying abroad. It provides a comprehensive directory of primary and secondary schools in Lithuania, both public and private, with details on the educational programs offered and contact information. This can be particularly helpful for parents who may be unfamiliar with the Lithuanian education system and looking for suitable schools for their children. The website also provides general information about the Lithuanian education system, including enrollment requirements, the structure of the curriculum, and available scholarships and grants. This can help parents navigate the system and understand the options available to them.

The website https://gamtosdraugai.lt/ can be a valuable resource for parents whose children have returned to Lithuania after studying abroad, particularly if they are interested in promoting environmental awareness and sustainable living practices. Overall, the website can be a useful tool for parents who want to help their children integrate into Lithuanian society and promote values of environmental awareness and sustainability.
